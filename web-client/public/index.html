~ Welcome to Tongue Monitor ~

Instructions:
  1. Take a photo or upload your file.
  2. Press diagnose or submit button to get your result.
  3. If tongue and tip are detected, you will get a result with tongue angle, tongue length, and relative length.
  4. No detection will be marked on the photo either tongue or lip is not detected.
Example:
  1. Make sure that there is enough light in the photo.
  2. Zoom your camera to the right place (as example below).
  3. Capture a photo to begin.
Remarks:
  1. Positive angle represent a clockwise movement in the image/video from 6 P.M.
  2. Negative angle represent an anti-clockwise movement in the image/video from 6 P.M.
  3. Tongue length is in pixel unit.
  4. Ratio of Tongue length to Lip size tries to demonstrate the the relative length of tongue out of mouth.
Method 1: Take a photo of your tongue by device camera.
Method 2: Upload your photo or video and get results.

Note: This process may take a few minutes according to the file size.
