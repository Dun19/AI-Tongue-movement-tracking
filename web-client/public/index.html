Welcome to Tongue Monitor

This application aims to provide help to people who need tongue movement tracking.

Instructions:

1. Take a photo or upload your file.

2. Press diagnose or submit button to get your result.

3. If tongue and tip are detected, you will get a result with tongue angle, tongue length, and relative length

4. No detection will be marked on the photo in either tongue or lip is not detected.

Remarks:

1. Positive angle represent an clockwise movement from 6 P.M.
Negative angle represent an anti-clockwise movement from 6 P.M.

2. Tongue length is in pixel unit.

3. Ratio of Tongue length to Lip size tries to demonstrate the the relative length of tongue out of mouth.

Method 1: Take a photo of your tongue by device camera.

Method 2: Upload your photo or video and get results.

Note: This process may take a few minutes according to the file size.
